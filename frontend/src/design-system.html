Typography

Font Pairing

DM Serif Display - Heading Font

DM Sans pairs naturally with DM Serif Display. Both belong to the DM type family by Colophon Foundry, sharing proportions and design DNA. The serif brings artisan warmth for headings while the sans keeps body text clean and readable.

Heading: --font-heading: "DM Serif Display", Georgia, serif
Body: --font-body: "DM Sans", system-ui, sans-serif
Mono: --font-mono: "DM Mono", ui-monospace, monospace
