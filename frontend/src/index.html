Rooted in the Forest, Made for Your Home

Centrifungal started with a simple idea - that anyone should be able to grow fresh, gourmet mushrooms without a garden or any special equipment. Our logs are inoculated by hand in small batches using spawn we cultivate ourselves, then slowly colonised in controlled conditions before reaching you.

The result? Mushrooms that taste like they were foraged minutes ago.
