About Centrifungal

A passion project born in the woods, grown with care, and shipped to your door.

Our Story

Centrifungal started the way a lot of good things do - with curiosity and a bit of spare timber. What began as a weekend experiment inoculating oak logs in a damp corner of a British woodland quickly turned into something more. The first shiitake flush was a revelation: fresh, fragrant, and utterly unlike anything from a supermarket shelf.

We realised that if growing gourmet mushrooms could bring us this much joy, it could do the same for others. So we set about making it as simple as possible - no specialist equipment, no sterile labs, no confusing jargon. Just a carefully prepared log that does the hard work for you.

Today, every Centrifungal log is still inoculated by hand in small batches. We cultivate our own spawn, source our hardwood locally, and take the time to let each log colonise fully before it reaches you. It is slower than factory methods, but the results speak for themselves.

What We Believe

We think everyone should have access to fresh, high-quality food that they have had a hand in growing. Mushrooms are a brilliant place to start - they do not need sunlight, a garden, or green fingers. Just a bit of moisture and patience.

We are not trying to be the biggest mushroom business in the country. We would rather stay small, do things properly, and make sure every log we send out is something we are genuinely proud of.

Our Values

Quality First

Every log is hand-checked before dispatch. If it does not meet our standard, it does not ship. We would rather delay an order than send something subpar.

Locally Sourced

Our hardwood comes from sustainably managed British woodlands. We keep our supply chain short and our footprint small.

Honest and Open

No gimmicks, no greenwashing. We tell you exactly what is in your log, how to look after it, and what to expect. If something goes wrong, we will make it right.
