What happens next

  1. 1

    Confirmation email

    You will receive an order confirmation email within the next few minutes. It includes a summary of your items, delivery address, and order reference number. Check your spam folder if it does not arrive.

  2. 2

    We prepare your order

    Our team hand-packs every order in compostable, plastic-free packaging designed to keep your grow logs safe and happy in transit. Most orders are dispatched within 1-2 working days.

  3. 3

    Shipping confirmation with tracking

    Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a second email with a tracking number so you can follow your parcel to your door. UK delivery typically takes 2-3 working days.

  4. 4

    Start growing

    When your logs arrive, follow our care instructions to get started. Most logs produce their first flush of mushrooms within 2-4 weeks.
