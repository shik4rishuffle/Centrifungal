Products

We offer a range of gourmet species including oyster mushrooms (various colours), shiitake, and lion's mane. Each product listing specifies the species. All are edible, culinary-grade varieties.

Standard grow logs are roughly 20-25cm long and 10-15cm in diameter - about the size of a large water bottle. They fit easily on a kitchen worktop, shelf, or windowsill. Exact dimensions vary slightly as each log is a natural piece of wood.

We use sustainably sourced hardwood and cultivate our spawn in-house without synthetic chemicals. While we have not pursued formal organic certification (it is expensive for a small business), our methods are as natural as they come.

A grow log is a piece of real hardwood that has been inoculated with mushroom spawn. It produces multiple flushes over several months and has a natural, rustic look. A grow kit typically uses a bag of substrate (like sawdust or straw) and tends to produce fewer flushes. We focus on logs because they last longer and look better in your home.

Our logs contain mushroom mycelium and hardwood - no nuts, dairy, gluten, or other common allergens. However, if you have a known mushroom allergy, these products are not suitable for you. The mushrooms produced are the same species sold in shops and farmers' markets.

Returns

Because grow logs are living biological products, we cannot accept returns for change of mind. However, if your log arrives damaged, contaminated, or fails to fruit within 6 weeks despite following our care instructions, we will replace it or issue a full refund.

Once we have approved a refund, it is processed within 2 working days. Depending on your bank, it may take another 3-5 working days to appear in your account.

First, check our care instructions to make sure conditions are right. If your log has not fruited after 6 weeks of proper care, email us and we will troubleshoot with you. If we cannot get it going, we will send a replacement free of charge.
